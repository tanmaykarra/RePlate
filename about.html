"Welcome to RePlate "




About us

We are a team of passionate individuals committed to addressing the issue of food waste in India. Our goal is to create a system that connects food donors with charities and NGOs, while also reducing the environmental impact of food waste.



How It Works


Step 1

Donors list surplus food on the platform.

Step 2

NGOs and charities request the food.

Step 3

Pickup is arranged & food is transported.

Step 4

Food reaches people in need & reduces waste.

Our Location


About usThe basic concept of this project Replate is to collect the excess/leftover food from donors such as hotels, restaurants, marriage halls , etc and distribute to the needy people .
